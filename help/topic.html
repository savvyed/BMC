How to join a video visit on your phone

▶ How-to clip
Text panel — translated steps appear here

Follow these steps

  1. Open the MyChart app on your phone.
  2. Tap Appointments at the bottom of the screen.
  3. Find your video visit in the list. It will show today's date and time.
  4. Tap Begin Visit. This button appears up to 30 minutes before your visit.
  5. Allow MyChart to use your camera and microphone if asked.
  6. Wait in the waiting room. Your provider will join when they are ready.
